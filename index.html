Calypso

Calypso is a mostly imperative language with some functional influences that is focused on flexibility and simplicity.

Note that Calypso is still in its early stages of development. The implementation so far is VERY incomplete and is definitely neither working nor ready for production. Contributions, however, are welcome and highly encouraged as they help to bring Calypso to life.
